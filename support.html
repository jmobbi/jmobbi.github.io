FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Are jmobbi apps free to download?

Yes. Every jmobbi app is free to download on the App Store, so you can get started right away without any upfront cost.

Are jmobbi apps safe to use?

Absolutely. Our apps use secure SDKs and encrypted connections, and your login details are never stored or seen by the app. Where a cloud service is involved, you sign in on that service's own official page, and the app keeps only a secure access token on your device, which you can revoke at any time. Uninstalling an app or disconnecting your account removes that token and any downloaded files.

Which devices do jmobbi apps support?

jmobbi apps are built natively for Apple devices. Most run on iPhone, iPad and Mac from a single download, with a layout that adapts to each screen size. Check the individual app's App Store page for its exact device support.

Which cloud services do your music players work with?

Each music player connects to one cloud service: Musicbox for Dropbox, GoPlayer for Google Drive, MPlayer for MEGA.NZ, YaPlayer for Yandex.Disk and FirePlayer for MediaFire. Money Currency Converter is a standalone currency converter and doesn't use cloud storage.

Can I listen to my music offline?

Yes. Download any track or playlist from your cloud storage and play it offline on flights, commutes or anywhere without a connection.

Which audio formats are supported?

Our music players handle all the common formats with no conversion required, including MP3, AAC, ALAC, FLAC, WAV, AIFF and M4R.

Do the apps include an equalizer?

Yes. The music players include a built-in audio equalizer with presets and a bass booster, so you can shape bass, treble and midrange to your taste.

Can I stream to Chromecast, AirPlay or Sonos?

Yes. You can cast your music to AirPlay, Chromecast and Sonos devices to fill any room with sound.

How is my privacy protected?

We keep data collection to a minimum and never store your cloud credentials. Access tokens live only on your device and can be revoked whenever you like. For full details, read our Privacy Policy.

How do I get help or suggest a feature?

We'd love to hear from you. Email support@jmobbi.com with any question, issue or feature request and our team will get back to you as soon as possible.

Still need a hand? Contact us at support@jmobbi.com.
