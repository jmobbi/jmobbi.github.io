FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Is Musicbox free to download?

Yes. Musicbox is a free download on the App Store and runs on iPhone, iPad and Mac.

What is Musicbox?

Musicbox is a music player built for the songs you keep in Dropbox. You can stream tracks on demand or download them for offline playback, build playlists, and shape the sound with the built-in equalizer. Your library is neatly organized by artist, album, genre and composer, while gapless playback, crossfade, Chromecast and AirPlay keep the listening seamless.

How does Musicbox work?

Once you link your Dropbox account, Musicbox reads the audio files stored there so you can stream them or save them for offline listening. It supports a wide range of formats, lets you create playlists, adjust the equalizer and cast to other devices, and it can also play music you have imported directly onto your device.

Is Musicbox safe to use?

Yes. Musicbox relies on secure SDKs and encrypted connections, and it never stores your login credentials. Signing in happens entirely on Dropbox's own official pages, and only a secure authorization token is kept on your device. You can revoke that token at any time from your Dropbox account settings in a web browser. Downloaded music lives in the app's Documents folder and can be removed through the built-in file manager, and disconnecting your account or uninstalling the app clears the stored token along with any downloaded data.

Which audio formats does Musicbox support?

Musicbox plays MP3, AAC, ALAC, FLAC, WAV, AIFF and M4R, so there is no need to convert your files first.

How do I create a playlist in Musicbox?
  1. Go to the Playlists section.
  2. Tap the "+" button, or open the "..." menu and choose New Playlist.
  3. Give the playlist a name and tap Save.
  4. Select the tracks you want to include and confirm.
How do I add songs to a playlist?
  1. Open the playlist you want to expand.
  2. Tap the "..." menu and choose Add Songs.
  3. Pick the tracks you want to add.
  4. Tap Done to save your changes.
How do I change the order of songs in a playlist?
  1. Open the Playlists section and select a playlist.
  2. Tap the "..." menu and choose Edit Playlist.
  3. Drag the tracks to reorder them, or sort them by name or size.
How do I download music for offline playback?
  1. Connect your Dropbox account in the Network section.
  2. Open the folder that holds the music you want.
  3. Tap the "..." menu and choose Select.
  4. Pick your tracks and tap Download.
How do I play audio files directly from Dropbox?
  1. Open the Network section and connect your Dropbox account.
  2. Browse into the folder that contains your music.
  3. Tap a file to add it to the queue and start playing.
How do I manage files in Dropbox?

Open the Network section and tap the "..." menu to select files. From there you can rename, move, delete or download the items stored in your Dropbox.

How do I use the equalizer?
  1. Open the Audio Player screen.
  2. Tap the Equalizer icon.
  3. Turn the equalizer on.
  4. Choose a preset or fine-tune the sliders to taste.
How do I stream music to Chromecast or AirPlay?
  1. Open the Audio Player screen.
  2. Tap the Audio Speaker icon.
  3. Select your Chromecast or AirPlay device.
How do I enable the Sleep Timer?
  1. Open Settings.
  2. Go to Audio Player.
  3. Tap Sleep Timer and choose an interval.
How do I configure gapless playback?

Open Settings, go to Audio Player and turn on Gapless Playback. Availability depends on your device and iOS version.

How can I resume a playlist where I left off?

Open Settings, go to Audio Player and enable "Save Audio Player State". After that, tap "Continue Playback" to pick up right where you stopped.

How do I transfer music from my computer?

You have two options: use Wi-Fi Drive while your computer and device are on the same Wi-Fi network, or transfer files through iTunes/Finder File Sharing.

How do I use Wi-Fi Drive to transfer files?
  1. Open the Network section and choose Computer.
  2. Select Connect using Wi-Fi and turn it on.
  3. Open the address Musicbox shows you in a desktop web browser.
  4. Drag and drop your files to upload them.
  5. Choose Stop Wi-Fi Drive when you are finished.
How do I move my existing music library to Dropbox?
  1. Sign up for a Dropbox account.
  2. Install the Dropbox desktop app on your computer.
  3. Upload your music files to your Dropbox.
  4. Sign in to Musicbox to access them.
How can I delete my music library?

Open Settings, go to Music Library and tap Delete Music Library. This only clears the local database inside the app; it does not remove your files in Dropbox or the tracks you have already downloaded.
