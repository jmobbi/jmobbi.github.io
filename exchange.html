FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Is Money Currency Converter free to download?

Yes, Money Currency Converter is free to download on the App Store for iPhone, iPad and Mac.

What is Money Currency Converter?

Money Currency Converter is a fast, easy-to-use currency converter for iPhone, iPad and Mac. It converts more than 160 world currencies with up-to-date exchange rates, works offline, handles batch conversions, and keeps your most-used currencies close at hand, making it ideal for travel, online shopping and everyday financial planning.

How does the app work?

Pick the currency you have and the currency you want, type in an amount, and the converted value appears instantly. The app refreshes exchange rates in the background so your conversions stay current, and it keeps the latest rates on your device so you can keep converting even without a connection.

How many currencies can I convert?

The app supports more than 160 world currencies, covering virtually every currency you are likely to need for travel, business or shopping.

Can I convert currencies offline?

Yes. The app saves the most recently downloaded exchange rates, so you can keep converting on flights, in remote areas or anywhere without an internet connection. Reconnect whenever you like to pull in the newest rates.

How accurate are the exchange rates?

Rates are updated regularly throughout the day to track the latest market data and refresh automatically when you open the app. Because rates constantly fluctuate and providers may differ slightly, the app is intended for everyday reference rather than for executing financial trades.

How do I refresh the exchange rates manually?
  1. Open the converter screen.
  2. Pull down on the list, or tap the refresh icon.
  3. The app downloads the latest rates and updates every conversion instantly.
How do I convert one amount into several currencies at once?

Enter an amount in your base currency and the app shows its value across all the currencies on your list at the same time, a batch conversion that is perfect for planning a multi-country trip or comparing prices.

How do I add or remove currencies from my list?
  1. Tap the "+" button to open the currency picker.
  2. Search for a currency by name or code and tap to add it.
  3. Swipe a currency in your list to remove it, or use edit mode to manage several at once.
How do I set my base currency?

Tap the currency you want to convert from, or enter an amount next to it, and the app treats it as the base, recalculating every other currency in your list against it.

How do I reorder my currencies?
  1. Open edit mode from the converter screen.
  2. Drag the handle next to each currency to move your favorites to the top.
  3. Save, and your preferred order is remembered next time you open the app.
Can I mark favorite currencies for quick access?

Yes. Keep the currencies you use most at the top of your list so they are always one glance away, and hide the ones you do not need to keep the screen focused.

Does the app include a built-in calculator?

Yes. You can type expressions such as sums or discounts directly into the amount field and convert the result, which is handy for splitting bills or working out totals while you shop abroad.

Which currencies and symbols are supported?

The app covers major, minor and many regional currencies, each shown with its ISO code (for example USD, EUR, GBP, JPY) and symbol so you can always tell exactly which currency you are looking at.

Do I need an account to use the app?

No account or sign-up is required. Download the app and start converting right away, with no registration, no email and no personal details needed.

Does the app work on iPad and Mac?

Yes. Money Currency Converter is a universal app that runs on iPhone, iPad and Mac, with a layout that adapts to each screen size.

Is my data private?

The app only needs a network connection to download exchange rates. It does not require an account and does not collect personal information to convert currencies. For full details, see our Privacy Policy.

Why do rates differ slightly from my bank?

Banks, card networks and exchange providers apply their own spreads and fees, so their rates can differ a little from the mid-market rates shown in the app. Use the app for quick, reliable estimates and confirm the exact figure with your provider before a large transaction.
