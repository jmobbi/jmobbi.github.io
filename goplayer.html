FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Is GoPlayer free to download?

Yes. GoPlayer is completely free to download from the App Store and works on iPhone, iPad and Mac.

What is GoPlayer?

GoPlayer is a music player that streams and downloads the songs you keep in Google Drive. You can listen offline, build playlists and shape your sound with the built-in equalizer, while your library is neatly organized by artist, album, genre and composer. It also supports gapless playback, crossfade and casting to Chromecast or AirPlay.

How does GoPlayer work?

Once you connect your Google Drive account, GoPlayer lets you stream tracks instantly or download them for offline listening. It plays a wide range of formats, lets you create playlists, fine-tune audio through the equalizer, cast to your speakers or TV, and even import local files stored on your device.

Is GoPlayer safe to use?

Yes. GoPlayer relies on secure SDKs and encrypted connections, and it never stores your account credentials. Sign-in happens on Google's own official pages, and the app keeps only a secure authorization token on your device. You can revoke that token at any time from your Google account in any web browser. Downloaded music lives in the app's Documents folder and can be removed with a file manager, and disconnecting the account or uninstalling the app clears the tokens and downloads.

Does GoPlayer keep my data private?

Yes. Any data obtained through Google APIs is handled in line with the Google API Services User Data Policy, including its Limited Use requirements. You can read the full details in our Privacy Policy.

Which audio formats does GoPlayer support?

GoPlayer plays MP3, AAC, ALAC, FLAC, WAV, AIFF and M4R with no conversion needed.

How do I create a playlist in GoPlayer?
  1. Go to the Playlists tab.
  2. Tap the "+" button, or tap "..." and choose New Playlist.
  3. Enter a name for your playlist.
  4. Tap Save.
  5. Select the tracks you want to include and confirm.
How do I add songs to a playlist?
  1. Open the playlist you want to update.
  2. Tap the "..." button.
  3. Choose Add Songs.
  4. Select the tracks you want and tap Done.
How do I change the order of songs in a playlist?
  1. Go to Playlists and open the playlist.
  2. Tap the "..." button and choose Edit Playlist.
  3. Drag tracks to reorder them, or use the sort option to arrange them automatically.
How do I download music for offline playback?
  1. Connect your Google Drive account from the Network tab.
  2. Open the folder that holds your music.
  3. Tap the "..." button and choose Select.
  4. Pick the tracks you want and tap Download.
How do I play audio files directly from Google Drive?
  1. Open the Network tab and connect your Google Drive account.
  2. Browse into the folder that contains your songs.
  3. Tap a file to add it to the queue and start playback.
How do I manage files in Google Drive?
  1. Open the Network tab and connect to Google Drive.
  2. Tap the "..." button to select files.
  3. From there you can rename, move, delete or download the selected items.
How do I use the equalizer?
  1. Open the Audio Player.
  2. Tap the Equalizer icon.
  3. Switch the equalizer on.
  4. Choose a ready-made preset or move the sliders to create your own custom sound.
How do I stream music to Chromecast or AirPlay?
  1. Open the Audio Player.
  2. Tap the Audio Speaker icon.
  3. Select your Chromecast or AirPlay device from the list.
How do I enable the Sleep Timer?
  1. Open Settings.
  2. Go to Audio Player.
  3. Tap Sleep Timer and choose your preferred interval.
How do I configure gapless playback?

Open Settings, go to Audio Player and switch on Gapless Playback. Please note that gapless support depends on your device and iOS version.

How can I resume a playlist where I left off?

Open Settings, go to Audio Player and turn on Save Audio Player State. After that, you can tap Continue Playback to pick up right where you stopped.

How do I transfer music from my computer?

You have two options. Use Wi-Fi Drive to move files over the same Wi-Fi network, or transfer them through iTunes or Finder File Sharing when your device is connected to your computer.

How do I use Wi-Fi Drive to transfer files?
  1. Open the Network tab and choose Computer.
  2. Select Connect using Wi-Fi and enable it.
  3. Open the shown URL in a browser on your desktop.
  4. Drag and drop your files into the browser window.
  5. When finished, tap Stop Wi-Fi Drive.
How do I move my existing music library to Google Drive?
  1. Sign up for a Google Drive account.
  2. Install the Google Drive desktop app on your computer.
  3. Upload your music files to Google Drive.
  4. Sign in to that same account in GoPlayer to access your library.
How can I delete my music library?

Open Settings, go to Music Library and tap Delete Music Library. This only clears the local database inside GoPlayer; it does not touch your files in Google Drive or any music you have already downloaded.
