GoPlayer: Your Cloud Music Companion for iOS and MacOS


Introducing GoPlayer, your go-to music app for iOS and macOS, specially designed to seamlessly integrate with Google Drive. Experience a harmonious blend of convenience and audio excellence as you dive into a world of music.

Privacy

GoPlayer respects your privacy. Our use and handling of data obtained from Google APIs adhere to Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. For more details, please review our Privacy Policy.

Universal Audio Support

GoPlayer is built to handle a wide range of audio formats effortlessly, including MP3, FLAC, AAC, M4A, WAV, AIFF, and M4R. Whether you're streaming, downloading, or organizing, GoPlayer ensures compatibility with all your favorite music files. Enjoy seamless playback without the hassle of converting formats, making it the perfect solution for audiophiles with diverse music libraries.

Audio Equalizer with Bass Booster

Enhance your listening experience with GoPlayer's powerful audio equalizer and bass booster. Adjust the sound to your liking with precision controls that let you fine-tune bass, treble, and midrange frequencies. Whether you prefer deep bass for your favorite tracks or a balanced sound for podcasts, GoPlayer adapts to your unique preferences.

Offline Mode

Never let poor connectivity interrupt your music. With GoPlayer, you can download your favorite tracks and playlists for offline listening. Perfect for flights, commutes, or any time you're on the go, this feature ensures your music is always within reach, even without an internet connection.

Create Playlists

Organize your music your way with GoPlayer’s intuitive playlist creation tools. Quickly group songs by mood, genre, or occasion, and keep your music collection perfectly curated. Whether you're preparing for a workout, a road trip, or a relaxing evening, GoPlayer makes it easy to create and manage playlists tailored to your needs.

Music Library

Effortlessly access your entire music collection with GoPlayer's intuitive library interface. Browse by artist, album, or track, and enjoy a seamless experience as you navigate your organized library. The user-friendly design ensures you spend less time searching and more time enjoying your music.

Audio Bookmarks

Never lose your place in a podcast or audiobook again. GoPlayer’s audio bookmarks let you mark specific points in a track, so you can easily return to where you left off. This feature is ideal for long-form content, ensuring a smooth and convenient listening experience.

Playback Speed Control

Take control of your listening experience with adjustable playback speeds. Slow down tracks to focus on details or speed them up to save time. GoPlayer gives you the flexibility to tailor your audio to your specific preferences, making it perfect for podcasts, audiobooks, and music alike.

Sleep Timer

Drift off to sleep with your favorite tunes using GoPlayer's sleep timer. Set a timer, and the app will automatically stop playback when it reaches the end. It's perfect for winding down at night, ensuring a peaceful transition to sleep while saving battery life.

Background Playback

Keep your music playing, no matter what you’re doing. With GoPlayer, you can enjoy uninterrupted playback while using other apps or even when your device is locked. Stay connected to your favorite tracks, podcasts, or audiobooks without missing a beat.

File Manager

Stay organized with GoPlayer’s integrated file manager. Quickly locate, move, and manage your audio files directly within the app. The streamlined interface makes it easy to keep your music files neatly arranged, saving you time and effort.

Advanced Search

Find exactly what you’re looking for with GoPlayer's advanced search feature. Whether you’re searching for a specific track, artist, or album, the robust search tool delivers instant results, making it easier than ever to locate your music.

Universal App

GoPlayer is designed for both iOS and macOS, offering a seamless music experience across devices. Enjoy the convenience of cloud integration with Google Drive, allowing you to access your music anytime, anywhere. It’s your music, your way.
