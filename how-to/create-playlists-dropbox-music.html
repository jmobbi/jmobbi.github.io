How to Make Playlists for Music Stored on Dropbox

3 min read · iPhone · iPad · Mac

If your music library lives in Dropbox, you don't have to shuffle through folders every time you want to listen. You can group your favourite tracks into playlists and play them your way on your iPhone, iPad or Mac. Here's how to build playlists with Musicbox, the dedicated Dropbox music player.

What you'll need

  • An iPhone, iPad or Mac.
  • A Dropbox account with your music files (MP3, FLAC, AAC, M4A, WAV, AIFF or M4R).
  • The free Musicbox app.

Step-by-step: create playlists from your Dropbox music

  1. Install Musicbox and connect your Dropbox. Download Musicbox and open it. Go to the Network section, choose Dropbox and sign in on Dropbox's official page. Musicbox only keeps a secure token on your device — your password is never stored.
  2. Open the Playlists section. Tap through to the Playlists section in Musicbox.
  3. Create a new playlist. Tap the "+" button, or tap "..." and choose New Playlist.
  4. Name it and save. Enter a name for your playlist and tap Save.
  5. Add songs. Tap "...", choose Add Songs, pick the tracks from your library, then tap Done.
  6. Reorder and refine. Tap "..." and choose Edit Playlist to drag and reorder tracks or sort them into the perfect running order.

Tip: Build playlists around the way you actually listen — by mood, genre or occasion. A focus mix for work, an upbeat set for the gym or a mellow playlist for winding down all pull from the same Dropbox library.

Why organize your Dropbox music into playlists?

Playlists turn a big Dropbox library into something you can enjoy in seconds, with no scrolling through folders. Once a playlist is built it stays ready to go, and it pairs perfectly with Musicbox's equalizer, sleep timer and background playback for the ideal listening experience.

Frequently asked questions

Is Musicbox free?

Yes. Musicbox is free to download and lets you connect Dropbox, create playlists and play your music on iPhone, iPad and Mac.

How many playlists can I create in Musicbox?

There is no set limit. You can create as many playlists as you like from your Dropbox music library and organize them by mood, genre or occasion.

How do I add or remove songs from a playlist?

Open the playlist, tap the "..." button and choose Add Songs to pick tracks from your library. To remove a track, choose Edit Playlist and delete it. Your Dropbox files stay untouched.

Can I reorder tracks in a playlist?

Yes. Tap "..." and choose Edit Playlist, then drag tracks up or down or sort them to set the perfect running order.

Does creating a playlist change or move my files in Dropbox?

No. Playlists are just references to your tracks. Creating, editing or deleting a playlist never moves, renames or deletes the original files stored in your Dropbox.

Can I play playlists offline?

Yes. Download the tracks in Musicbox first, and you can play your playlists offline without an internet connection.

Does Musicbox work on iPhone, iPad and Mac?

Yes. Musicbox works on iPhone, iPad and Mac, so you can build and enjoy your Dropbox playlists across all your Apple devices.
