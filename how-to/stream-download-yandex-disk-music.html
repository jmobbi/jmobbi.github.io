How to Stream Music from Yandex.Disk and Download It for Offline

4 min read · iPhone · iPad · Mac

Your Yandex.Disk can be the home for your entire music collection — and you don't have to download it all to enjoy it. With the right app you can stream any track instantly, then save your favourites for offline listening on a flight, a subway ride or a road trip. Here's how to do it with YaPlayer, the dedicated Yandex.Disk music player.

What you'll need

  • An iPhone, iPad or Mac.
  • A Yandex.Disk account with your music files (MP3, AAC, ALAC, FLAC, WAV, AIFF or M4R).
  • The free YaPlayer app.

Step-by-step: stream and download Yandex.Disk music

  1. Install YaPlayer. Download YaPlayer free from the App Store and open it.
  2. Connect your Yandex.Disk. Go to the Network section, choose Yandex.Disk and sign in on Yandex's official page. YaPlayer only keeps a secure token on your device — your password is never stored.
  3. Browse to your music. Open the folder that holds the songs, albums or playlists you want to hear.
  4. Stream a track. Tap any track to start streaming it instantly straight from Yandex.Disk — no download required.
  5. Download for offline. To keep music offline, tap the "..." button, choose Select, pick the tracks you want and tap Download. YaPlayer saves them into the app.
  6. Play offline. Switch to Airplane Mode to test it — your downloaded music keeps playing perfectly with no connection.

Tip: Downloaded tracks live in YaPlayer's own storage. To free up space later, open the built-in file manager and delete the files you no longer need — your originals stay safe in Yandex.Disk.

Why stream and download?

Streaming lets you reach a huge Yandex.Disk library without filling up your device, while downloads give you zero buffering, no data usage and gapless playback even where there's no coverage. Together they're the most flexible way to enjoy your music on the go, and they pair perfectly with YaPlayer's equalizer, sleep timer and background playback.

Frequently asked questions

Is YaPlayer free?

Yes. YaPlayer is free to download from the App Store on iPhone, iPad and Mac, and you can stream and download your Yandex.Disk music with it at no cost.

Can I stream Yandex.Disk music without downloading it?

Yes. Tap any track in YaPlayer to stream it instantly from Yandex.Disk — nothing is saved to your device unless you choose to download it.

How do I download Yandex.Disk tracks for offline listening?

Tap the "..." button, choose Select, pick the tracks you want and tap Download. YaPlayer saves them into the app so you can play them offline.

Does offline playback work with no internet?

Yes. Once tracks are downloaded they play with no connection at all — you can test it by switching your device to Airplane Mode.

Which audio formats does YaPlayer support?

YaPlayer plays MP3, AAC, ALAC, FLAC, WAV, AIFF and M4R files stored on your Yandex.Disk.

Is my Yandex login kept private and can I revoke access?

Yes. You sign in on Yandex's official page and only a secure token is stored on your device — your password is never saved. You can revoke access at any time from your Yandex account's connected apps settings.

Can I stream Yandex.Disk music over cellular?

Yes. YaPlayer streams over Wi-Fi or cellular. To avoid mobile data usage, download tracks while on Wi-Fi and play them offline.

Does YaPlayer work on iPhone, iPad and Mac?

Yes. YaPlayer runs on iPhone, iPad and Mac, so you can stream and download your Yandex.Disk music on all your Apple devices.
