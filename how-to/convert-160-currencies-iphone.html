How to Convert 160+ Currencies on iPhone Fast and Easy

3 min read · iPhone · iPad · Mac

Whether you're planning a trip abroad or checking a price on an overseas online store, you need accurate exchange rates without any hassle. The good news: you can convert 160+ world currencies right on your iPhone, iPad or Mac in seconds. Here's how to do it with Money Currency Converter, a fast and simple currency converter with real-time rates and offline support.

What you'll need

  • An iPhone, iPad or Mac.
  • The currencies you want to convert (any of 160+ supported, from USD and EUR to GBP and JPY).
  • The free Money Currency Converter app — no account needed.

Step-by-step: convert 160+ currencies fast

  1. Install Money Currency Converter. Download Money Currency Converter free from the App Store and open it.
  2. Add the currencies you use. Tap the "+" button and add the currencies you need. Search by name or ISO code such as USD, EUR, GBP or JPY.
  3. Enter an amount. Type an amount next to any currency and every other currency on your list updates instantly — batch conversion means one number converts to all of them at once.
  4. Reorder your list. Drag your currencies to keep the ones you use most at the top, so your favorites are always the first thing you see.
  5. Convert offline. Rates refresh automatically and are stored on your device for offline use, so you can keep converting even without a connection.

Tip: The rates shown are for everyday reference and may differ slightly from the rate your bank or card provider applies at the point of sale.

Why use a dedicated currency converter?

A purpose-built converter is faster than searching the web every time and keeps working when you're offline. It's ideal for travel, budgeting a trip, or comparing prices while shopping online — no account, no sign-up, just quick and accurate conversions across 160+ currencies whenever you need them.

Frequently asked questions

Is Money Currency Converter free?

Yes. Money Currency Converter is free to download from the App Store and lets you convert 160+ currencies with no account or sign-up required.

How many currencies can I convert?

You can convert between 160+ world currencies, from major ones like USD, EUR, GBP and JPY to less common regional currencies. Search by name or ISO code to add any of them.

Does Money Currency Converter work offline?

Yes. The latest exchange rates are stored on your device, so you can keep converting currencies offline without an internet connection — ideal when travelling abroad.

How often do exchange rates update and are they real-time?

Rates refresh automatically whenever you're online, giving you up-to-date real-time exchange rates. The most recent rates are then saved for offline conversions.

Do I need an account to use Money Currency Converter?

No. There is no sign-up and no account needed. Just download the app, add your currencies and start converting right away.

Can I convert one amount into many currencies at once?

Yes. Enter an amount next to any currency and every other currency on your list updates instantly. This batch conversion converts one number into all your chosen currencies at the same time.

Why might the rate differ slightly from my bank?

The app shows mid-market reference rates for everyday use. Your bank or card provider may add a margin or fee at the point of sale, so their final rate can differ slightly.

Does it work on iPhone, iPad and Mac?

Yes. Money Currency Converter works on iPhone, iPad and Mac, so you can convert 160+ currencies on whichever Apple device you're using.
