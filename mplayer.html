FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Is MPlayer free to download?

Yes. MPlayer is completely free to download from the App Store and runs on both iPhone and iPad.

What is MPlayer?

MPlayer is a dedicated music player for your MEGA.NZ account. It lets you stream your tracks on demand or download them for offline listening, build playlists, and shape your sound with a built-in equalizer. Your library is neatly organized by artist, album, genre and composer, and you get extras like gapless playback, crossfade, and streaming to Chromecast and AirPlay.

How does MPlayer work?

Once you connect your MEGA.NZ account, MPlayer gives you full access to the music stored there.

  • Stream any song instantly, or download it to keep for offline playback.
  • Play a wide range of audio formats without converting anything first.
  • Organize your music into playlists and fine-tune the sound with the equalizer.
  • Cast to Chromecast or AirPlay, and even import audio files stored locally on your device.
Is MPlayer safe to use?

Yes. MPlayer relies on secure SDKs and encrypted connections, and your MEGA.NZ credentials are never stored inside the app. All sign-in happens on MEGA.NZ's own official pages, after which a secure authorization token is kept on your device to maintain the connection. You can revoke that token at any time from your MEGA.NZ account in a web browser. Any music you download is saved in the app's own Documents folder and can be removed with a file manager. Disconnecting your account or uninstalling MPlayer removes the stored tokens and downloaded files.

Which audio formats does MPlayer support?

MPlayer plays MP3, AAC, ALAC, FLAC, WAV, AIFF and M4R with no conversion needed.

How do I create a playlist in MPlayer?
  1. Open the Playlists section.
  2. Tap the "+" button, or tap "..." and choose New Playlist.
  3. Give your playlist a name and tap Save.
  4. Select the tracks you want to include and confirm.
How do I add songs to an existing playlist?
  1. Open the playlist you want to update.
  2. Tap "..." and choose Add Songs.
  3. Pick the tracks you'd like to add.
  4. Tap Done to save your changes.
How do I change the order of songs in a playlist?
  1. Go to Playlists and open the one you want to edit.
  2. Tap "..." and choose Edit Playlist.
  3. Drag tracks to rearrange them, or use a sort option to reorder automatically.
How do I download music for offline playback?
  1. Connect your MEGA.NZ account from the Network section.
  2. Open the folder that holds the music you want.
  3. Tap "..." and choose Select.
  4. Pick your tracks and tap Download.
How do I play audio files directly from MEGA.NZ?
  1. Go to the Network section and connect your MEGA.NZ account.
  2. Open the folder containing your music.
  3. Tap any file to add it to the queue and start playback.
How do I manage my files on MEGA.NZ?
  1. Open the Network section and connect MEGA.NZ.
  2. Tap "..." to select the items you want to manage.
  3. Rename, move, delete or download them as needed.
How do I use the equalizer?
  1. Open the Audio Player.
  2. Tap the Equalizer icon.
  3. Turn the equalizer on.
  4. Choose a ready-made preset or adjust the sliders to create your own sound.
How do I stream music to Chromecast or AirPlay?
  1. Open the Audio Player.
  2. Tap the Audio Speaker icon.
  3. Select your Chromecast or AirPlay device from the list.
How do I enable the Sleep Timer?
  1. Go to Settings.
  2. Open Audio Player.
  3. Tap Sleep Timer and choose the interval you'd like.
How do I configure gapless playback?

Open Settings, go to Audio Player, and turn on Gapless Playback. Please note that its availability can depend on your device and iOS version.

How can I resume a playlist where I left off?

Go to Settings, open Audio Player, and enable Save Audio Player State. After that, you can pick up right where you stopped by choosing Continue Playback.

How do I transfer music from my computer?

You have two options: use Wi-Fi Drive to move files over your local Wi-Fi network, or transfer them through iTunes/Finder File Sharing while your device is connected.

How do I use Wi-Fi Drive to transfer files?
  1. Open the Network section and choose Computer.
  2. Select Connect using Wi-Fi and enable it.
  3. Open the shown URL in a browser on your desktop, making sure both devices share the same Wi-Fi.
  4. Drag and drop your files into the browser window.
  5. Tap Stop Wi-Fi Drive when you're finished.
How do I move my existing music library to MEGA.NZ?
  1. Sign up for a MEGA.NZ account if you don't have one.
  2. Install the MEGA.NZ desktop app on your computer.
  3. Upload your music files to your MEGA.NZ storage.
  4. Sign in to MPlayer to access your whole library.
How can I delete my music library?

Go to Settings, open Music Library, and tap Delete Music Library. This only clears the local database on your device; it does not remove your files on MEGA.NZ or the music you've already downloaded.
