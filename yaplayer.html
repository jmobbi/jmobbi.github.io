FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Is YaPlayer free to download?

Yes. YaPlayer is completely free to download from the App Store and runs on iPhone, iPad and Mac.

What is YaPlayer?

YaPlayer is a dedicated music player for your Yandex.Disk library. It lets you stream tracks on demand or download them for offline listening, and it neatly organizes everything by artist, album, genre and composer. You also get playlists, a built-in equalizer, gapless playback, crossfade and the ability to cast to Chromecast or AirPlay.

How does YaPlayer work?

Once you connect your Yandex.Disk account, YaPlayer gives you direct access to your stored music. You can stream any file over the network or download it to keep for offline playback. It handles a wide range of audio formats, lets you build playlists, fine-tune sound with the equalizer, cast to speakers and TVs, and even import local files kept on your device.

Is YaPlayer safe to use?

Yes. YaPlayer relies on secure SDKs and encrypted connections, and it never stores your Yandex.Disk credentials. You sign in directly on Yandex's own official pages, and only a secure authorization token is kept on your device. You can revoke that token at any time from your Yandex.Disk account in a web browser. Any music you download lives in the app's own Documents folder and can be removed through the built-in file manager. Disconnecting your account or uninstalling the app clears all tokens and downloaded files.

Which audio formats does YaPlayer support?

YaPlayer plays MP3, AAC, ALAC, FLAC, WAV, AIFF and M4R with no conversion needed.

How do I create a playlist in YaPlayer?
  1. Open the Playlists section.
  2. Tap the "+" button, or tap "..." and choose New Playlist.
  3. Give your playlist a name and tap Save.
  4. Select the tracks you want to include and confirm your choice.
How do I add songs to a playlist?
  1. Open the playlist you want to expand.
  2. Tap the "..." button and choose Add Songs.
  3. Pick the tracks you'd like to add.
  4. Tap Done to save them to the playlist.
How do I change the order of songs in a playlist?
  1. Go to Playlists and open the one you want to rearrange.
  2. Tap "..." and select Edit Playlist.
  3. Drag the tracks into the order you prefer, or use the sort option.
How do I download music for offline playback?
  1. Connect your Yandex.Disk account in the Network section.
  2. Open the folder that holds the music you want.
  3. Tap "..." and choose Select.
  4. Pick your tracks and tap Download.
How do I play audio files directly from Yandex.Disk?
  1. Open the Network section and connect your Yandex.Disk account.
  2. Browse to the folder containing your music.
  3. Tap any file to add it to the queue and start playing.
How do I manage files in Yandex.Disk?
  1. Open the Network section and go to your Yandex.Disk.
  2. Tap "..." to select the files you want to work with.
  3. Choose to rename, move, delete or download them.
How do I use the equalizer?
  1. Open the Audio Player.
  2. Tap the Equalizer icon.
  3. Turn the equalizer on.
  4. Pick a ready-made preset or adjust the sliders to create your own sound.
How do I stream music to Chromecast or AirPlay?
  1. Open the Audio Player.
  2. Tap the Audio Speaker icon.
  3. Select your Chromecast or AirPlay device from the list.
How do I enable the Sleep Timer?
  1. Open Settings.
  2. Go to Audio Player.
  3. Tap Sleep Timer and choose the interval you want.
How do I configure gapless playback?

Open Settings, go to Audio Player and turn on Gapless Playback. Keep in mind that whether it works seamlessly can depend on your device and iOS version.

How can I resume a playlist where I left off?

In Settings, open Audio Player and turn on Save Audio Player State. After that, you can use the Continue Playback option to pick up right where you stopped.

How do I transfer music from my computer?

You have two options: use Wi-Fi Drive to move files while your computer and device are on the same Wi-Fi network, or transfer them through File Sharing in iTunes or Finder.

How do I use Wi-Fi Drive to transfer files?
  1. Open the Network section and tap Computer.
  2. Choose Connect using Wi-Fi and enable it.
  3. Open the shown URL in a browser on your desktop.
  4. Drag and drop your files into the browser window.
  5. When you're finished, tap Stop Wi-Fi Drive.
How do I move my existing music library to Yandex.Disk?
  1. Sign up for a Yandex.Disk account.
  2. Install the Yandex.Disk desktop app on your computer.
  3. Upload your music files to Yandex.Disk.
  4. Sign in to YaPlayer to access everything you uploaded.
How can I delete my music library?

Open Settings, go to Music Library and tap Delete Music Library. This only clears YaPlayer's local database. Your files on Yandex.Disk and any tracks you've downloaded stay untouched.
